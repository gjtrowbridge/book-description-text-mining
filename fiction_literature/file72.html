Winner of the Hugo, Nebula, British Science Fiction, Locus and Arthur C. Clarke Awards.


On a remote, icy planet, the soldier known as Breq is drawing closer to completing her quest.

Once, she was the Justice of Toren - a colossal starship with an artificial intelligence linking thousands of soldiers in the service of the Radch, the empire that conquered the galaxy.

Now, an act of treachery has ripped it all away, leaving her with one fragile human body, unanswered questions, and a burning desire for vengeance.

Winner of the 2014 Hugo Award for Best Novel
Winner of the 2013 Nebula Award for Best Novel
Winner of the 2014 Arthur C. Clarke Award
