Stone Barrington is back in an edge-of-your-seat adventure in the New York Times bestselling series.

After giving legal advice to his latest client, Stone Barrington thinks hes done with the man. But its soon clear that Stone hasnt seen the end of the case. Several people are keenly interested in this gentlemans activities and how they may relate to a long-ago crimeand some of them will stop at nothing to find the information they desire.

On a hunt that leads from Floridas tropical beaches to the posh vacation homes of the Northeast, Stone finds himself walking a tightrope between ambitious authorities and seedy lowlifes who all have the same prize in their sights. In this cutthroat contest of wills, its winner-takes-alland Stone will need every bit of his cunning and resourcefulness to be the last man standing.
