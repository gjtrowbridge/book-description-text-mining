The release of George Lucas's Star Wars in 1977 launched an epic saga that has changed how we view science fiction. Experience the wonder and magic of the Star Wars universe with The Star Wars Trilogy , a collection of the first three Star Wars novels. George Lucas provides an illuminating introduction for each novel.
In this collection are:
  • Star Wars: A New Hope by George Lucas
  • The Empire Strikes Back by Donald F. Glut
  • Return of the Jedi by James Kahn

The Star Wars Trilogy is a distinguished new addition to Barnes & Noble's Collectible Editions classics. Each volume features an exquisitely designed bonded leather binding, with distinctive gilt edging and an attractive silk-ribbon bookmark. Decorative, durable and collectible, these books offer hours of pleasure to readers young and old and are an indispensible cornerstone for every home library.
