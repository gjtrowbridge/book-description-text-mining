  Lieutenant Eve Dallas has plenty to be grateful for, especially Roarkes big Irish family, which is a joyful improvement on her own dark childhood.
 
 
  Other couples arent as lucky. The Reinholds, for example, are lying in their home stabbed and bludgeoned almost beyond recognition. Those who knew them are stunnedand heartbroken by the evidence that they were murdered by their own son.
 
 
  Turns out that twenty-six-year-old Jerry is not only capable of brutality but has taking a liking to it. With the money hes stolen from his parents and a long list of grievances, he intends to finally make his mark on the world. Eve and her team already know the who, how, and why of this murder. What they need to pinpoint is where Jerrys going to strike next.
