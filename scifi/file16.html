The pulse-pounding new Star Trek thriller from David Macka direct sequel to the New York Times bestselling series The Fall!

Amoral, shrouded in secrecy, and answerable to no one, Section 31 is the mysterious covert operations division of Starfleet, a rogue shadow group committed to safeguarding the Federation at any cost. Doctor Julian Bashir sacrificed his career for a chance to infiltrate Section 31 and destroy it from within. Now its asking him to help it stop the Breen from stealing a dangerous new technology from the Mirror Universeone that could give the Breen control over the galaxy. Its a mission Bashir cant refusebut is it really the shot hes been waiting for? Or is it a trap from which even his genetically enhanced intellect cant escape?
