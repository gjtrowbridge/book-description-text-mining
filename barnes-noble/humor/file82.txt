
 
  A
  
   New York Times
  
  bestseller? Oh, you know the dogs weren't going to let the cats get away with that! This canine companion to
  
   I Could Pee on This
  
  , the beloved volume of poems by cats,
  
   I Could Chew on This
  
  will have dog lovers laughing out loud. Doggie laureates not only chew on quite a lot of things, they also reveal their creativity, their hidden motives, and their eternal (and sometimes misguided) effervescence through such musings as "I Dropped a Ball," "I Lose My Mind When You Leave the House," and "Can You Smell That?" Accompanied throughout by portraits of the canine poets in all their magnificence,
  
   I Could Chew on This
  
  is a work of unbridled enthusiasm, insatiable appetite, and, yes, creative genius.
